What We Do

Core Electrical Services

From high-stakes commercial fit-ups to whole-home modernizations, IMC Electric delivers code-compliant work built to last — on schedule and on budget.

Commercial Tenant Fit-Ups & Retrofits

Complete electrical packages for retail storefronts, office spaces, restaurants, and schools across Downtown Ottawa and the ByWard Market. We handle panel distribution, lighting design, data rough-ins, and ESA sign-offs — so your space is move-in ready on opening day.

Electrical Service Upgrades

Safe, modern 100A to 200A+ panel upgrades for older homes in the Glebe, Westboro, and Vanier. We replace outdated fuse boxes, install new meter bases, and ensure your home meets today's power demands — including EV chargers, heat pumps, and modern kitchens.

EV Charger Infrastructure

Residential Level 2 charger installations and commercial DC fast-charger setups with smart-panel integration. We assess your existing load, handle ESA permits, and configure load management so your charging infrastructure is efficient, future-proof, and rebate-eligible.

Whole-Home Rewiring & Custom Renovations

Full code-compliant rewiring for custom builds and major renovations — including proper grounding, arc-fault protection, and modern circuit distribution. We work closely with your designer or GC to coordinate rough-ins, trims, and final inspections seamlessly.

Subcontracting for Builders & GCs

Reliable, predictable electrical division partnerships for large-scale developments and multi-family builds across Ottawa. We provide detailed quotes, maintain open communication with your site team, and deliver on schedule — every floor, every unit, every time.

Who We Are

The Installation & Maintenance Centre

IMC stands for Installation and Maintenance Centre — and that name reflects exactly how we operate. We are a dedicated hub for electrical installation and ongoing maintenance, built on the belief that every project — whether a restaurant fit-up in the ByWard Market or a full rewire in the Glebe — deserves the same level of precision, safety, and care.

Our team operates under direct Master Electrician oversight on every job. We hold ourselves to the highest ESA standards because the environments we work in — schools, commercial kitchens, healthcare-adjacent spaces, and family homes — demand nothing less.

But high standards don't mean a cold process. We believe great electrical work starts with open communication. Whether you're a general contractor coordinating a multi-unit build or a homeowner wondering if your panel can handle an EV charger, we encourage you to ask any question — no matter how basic it seems. We're here to explain, guide, and deliver work you'll feel confident about for years to come.
